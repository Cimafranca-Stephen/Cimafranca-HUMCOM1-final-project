Katsudon

Eggs
Pork Cutlet
Rice
Cabbage
Sauce of your choosing
Cooking Oil
Bread Crumbs
Flour

  1. First put some oil in a deep pan or pot and let it heat up
  2. Beat some eggs in a bowl or fry them on a separate pan [if you are going to fry you still need to beat eggs]
  3. Chop some onions and garlic
  4. After setting that aside use the beaten eggs to batter your Pork cutlet, Coat the cutlet with Egg
  5. Coat the Cutlet in batter and breadcrumbs
  6. Then Deep Fry until golden brown
  7. Preparing the egg you need a pan with oil in it, put the onions and garlic in the pan and let them simmer until brown
  8. Place your beaten eggs and thoroughly cook them
  9. Ready a bowl of rice, top it with the egg, and cut the pork cutlet into strips and top them over the egg
  10. top it with a sauce of your choice

Procedure
Fill a pot or deep pan with oil and heat it up, batter a pork cutlet with eggs, flour, and breadcrumbs.
Deep fry the cutlet, when it finishes prepare another pan with a bit of oil, that's when you let chopped
onions and garlic simmer on the pan, beat an egg and put it in the pan after the garlic and onions get a brownish colour.
Cook the eggs on medium heat and aim for a jammy interior consistency with a firm exterior texture
Put rice in a bowl and top it with the scrambled egg, chopped cabages, and then cut your pork cutlet into strips and
top them on your eggs.
